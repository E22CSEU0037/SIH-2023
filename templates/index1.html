    Andhra Pradesh

    5 / 5

    2023

    The Andhra Pradesh Government is promoting textile products in three districts under the 'One District One Product' program, aiming to develop traditional industrial hubs and enhance exports. The products will be geo-tagged for a competitive edge in global markets. Infrastructure, supply chain, and skill gaps are being addressed, with a focus on overseas markets. Financial and logistical support is provided, leveraging Andhra Pradesh's status as a major cotton and raw silk producer with a developed spinning and processing sector and an innovative apparel city in Visakhapatnam.

    The government of Arunachal Pradesh is actively promoting areca nut cultivation from Changlang district as part of its 'One District One Product' (ODOP) program. This initiative aims to develop areca nut as a key traditional product from the region and unlock its economic potential. Through targeted support, including infrastructure improvements and market integration, the government seeks to boost the livelihoods of local farmers and elevate Changlang district as a significant hub for areca nut production in the state.

    Assam

    5 / 5

    2023

    The government is actively promoting Assam Tea as a key product under the 'One District One Product' (ODOP) initiative, particularly from Dibrugarh in Assam. Grown in the fertile plains along the Brahmaputra River, with ideal climatic conditions and ample rainfall, Assam Tea is celebrated for its deep-amber hue and malty taste, making it an ideal choice for a refreshing morning beverage. This initiative aims to enhance the livelihoods of local tea growers and contribute to the economic development of the region.
